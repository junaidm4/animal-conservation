Our Projects

üå≥ Habitat Restoration

We are working on restoring deforested areas and degraded ecosystems to support the recovery of biodiversity and mitigate climate change.

Ongoing Projects

  • Protecting the Rainforests - Aiming to halt deforestation and preserve biodiversity.
  • Wildlife Conservation in Botswana - Working to reduce poaching and promote anti-poaching initiatives.
  • Coral Reef Restoration - Rebuilding coral ecosystems to restore marine life.

Success Stories

Saving Endangered Tigers

Thanks to our conservation efforts, tiger populations in protected areas have started to show signs of recovery. Local communities have also benefited from sustainable tourism.

Restoring Coastal Habitats

Our coastal restoration program has brought back vital marine life to areas that were once barren. The community-led effort is a key to its success.

Project Timeline

  • January 2024 - Launch of Tiger Conservation Program
  • March 2024 - Completed restoration of 50 hectares of coastal land
  • August 2024 - Expanded project to new locations
